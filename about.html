About

Detroit Color Grade is a boutique color studio providing color correction and grading services for commercials, film, TV, music videos, and more. You name it - HD, 4K, 6K, 8K, R3D, ARRIRAW, BRAW, ProRes, DNxHR, h.264/h.265 - we're equipped to handle any resolution and format you're working with.
‍
Origin: After providing a decade of color grading support for independent filmmakers, founder Grant Pichla launched Detroit Color Grade to bring his team's solutions to agencies, in-house corporate teams, production companies, and filmmakers. Based in Metro Detroit, the company is fully remote and has served clients well beyond Southeast Michigan: from Chicago, to New York City, to Atlanta, to Los Angeles.
